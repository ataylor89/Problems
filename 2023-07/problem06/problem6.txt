ΚΑΤΑ ΜΑΘΘΑΙΟΝ 1

1 Βίβλος γενέσεως Ἰησοῦ Χριστοῦ υἱοῦ Δαυὶδ υἱοῦ Ἀβραάμ.

The story of the birth of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.

Vocabulary
1. Βίβλος: Book, story; 2nd declension nominative singular
2. γενέσεως: Birth, generation; 3rd declension genitive singular
3. υἱός: Son; 2nd declension nominative singular
