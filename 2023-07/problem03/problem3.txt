Matthaeus Caput 1

1 Liber generationis Jesu Christi filii David, filii Abraham.

The story of the birth of Jesus Christ, son of David, son of Abraham.

Vocabulary:
Liber: Book, story; 2nd declension nominative singular
Generationis: Birth, generation; 3rd declension genitive singular
Filius: Son; 2nd declension nominative singular
